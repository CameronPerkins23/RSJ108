How to Create a Monogram...

Assembly time: 45 minutes
Usefulness rating: 100% useful

Monograms are really important in the business world today. They reflect your personal brand and give people a first impression of you. They're extremely helpful with making connections and aren't too difficult to make either!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Computer
  • Internet
  • Paper
  • Pencil or Pen

  1. Sketch rough drafts of your monogram on your paper.

    With your two initials and two main themes, start sketching rough drafts of your monogram. Pick the draft that you're most happy with.

  2. Login to Adobe Illustrator.

    Login to Illustrator and create a document by selecting New File on the top left corner of the screen. Make sure your document has dimensions of 800 x 800 pixels.

  3. Add your first initial.

    After creating your document, type the letter you'd like to be the largest in your monogram.

  4. Change the font.

    Change the font type to Lucida Handwriting. This font is included as a standard system font on Windows and Mac computers. You can use whatever font you'd like on this step.

  5. With the letters selected, change the font size to its largest size.

    When you increase the font size, the box doesn't always increase in size. Click and drag the corners of the box outward until you can see the whole letter. If you want the letter to be even larger, type a number, like 200, into the Font Size box.

  6. Add one more letter, but make its font size at least half the size of the first letter.

    When you increase the font size, the box doesn't always increase in size. Click and drag the corners of the box outward until you can see the whole letter. If you want the letter to be even larger, type a number, like 200, into the Font Size box.

  7. Click and drag the letters around until you like how they look. Then, select your initials.

    After your content with your monogram design, drag and press your mouse over both of your initials so that you can see their text outlines. Then left-click and press group. You now have a monogram!

  8. Creating outlines and pathfinders.

    Drag and press your mouse over your monogram and press Create outlines. Then go to the object tab at the top of your screen, and then press path and then offset path.

  9. Finally, export your completed monogram as a PNG.
